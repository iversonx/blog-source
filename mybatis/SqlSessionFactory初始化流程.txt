1. SqlSessionFactory初始化过程也是mybatis初始化过程，其内容是通过解析mybatis-config.xml文件来组装Configuration对象。mybaits所有的配置都封装在Configuration对象中。


mybatis配置元素与Configuration对象属性的对应

<properties> -> Properties variables

<settings>

<typeAliases> -> TypeAliasRegistry

<plugins> -> InterceptorChain interceptorChain

<objectFactory> -> ObjectFactory objectFactory

<objectWrapperFactory> -> ObjectWrapperFactory objectWrapperFactory

<reflectorFactory> -> ReflectorFactory reflectorFactory


<environments> -> Environment environment


<typeHandlers> -> TypeHandlerRegistry 

<mappers> -> MapperRegistry mapperRegistry


此过程SqlSessionFactoryBuilder最终交由XMLConfigBuilder来完成。XMLConfigBuilder在解析完配置文件后，会返回Configuration对象；
SqlSessionFactoryBuilder根据Configuration对象实例化一个DefaultSqlSessionFactory。
